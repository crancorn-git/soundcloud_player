 Window opens with dark UI, no visible menu bar.
Pass: No uncaught errors in console.

 Ctrl+K focuses global search field.
Pass: Caret in top search bar.

 Media keys (Play/Pause/Next/Prev) trigger footer controls.
Pass: Footer play button toggles; no crash.

2) Account flow (1 min)

 Right header shows “Sign in to SoundCloud”. Click it, complete login.
Pass: Account chip shows username + avatar.

 Click account chip → confirm Sign out → chip returns to Sign in.
Pass: Then sign in again (you’ll need it for import).

3) Importer: Likes → Playlists → Albums (3–4 min)

Click Sync Library… from the header.

 Import overlay appears with status text and non-zero progress within ~3–5s.
Pass: Progress increments visibly during scroll.

 When it says Collecting tracks…, watch console—no red errors.

 On completion: overlay closes; sidebar Likes count > 0.
Pass: Likes list is populated; clicking a row selects it.

Switch to Playlists tab:

 Playlist cards appear with titles; expanding shows track rows.
Pass: “Play all” plays first track; “Shuffle” starts random.

Switch to Albums tab:

 Album cards appear; if /you/albums was empty, verify items arrived via the fallback (username /albums).
Pass: Expand an album and play a track.

Guardrail: Ensure Likes never disappear after playlists/albums merge. Flip back to Likes—count must remain the same as after import finished.

4) Playback & queue (1 min)

 From Likes, double-click a row → track starts; footer shows title/artist; cover loads.
Pass: Time counter advances; seekbar moves.

 Right-click a different Like → Queue next.
Pass: Current plays to end, next queued plays automatically.

 Click ♡ in footer (local like toggle) → opacity changes.
Pass: No console errors; doesn’t remove from Likes list.

5) Search (1–2 min)

 Search something common (e.g., lofi beats).
Pass: Skeleton rows show while loading; results list populates.

 Click … on a result → Save to Likes.
Pass: Switch to Likes; the saved track is present.

 Play a search result → audio plays; Queue button adds it “next”.
Pass: Status tip briefly shows “Queued next”.

6) Persistence (1 min)

 Close the app window (not quit). Click tray icon → window toggles visible/hidden.

 Quit from tray → relaunch yarn start.
Pass: Last queue and volume restored; last track resumable; Likes/Playlists/Albums still present without re-import.
